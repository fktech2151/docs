## Set up

Click this [click this link](https://cdn.papercut.com/web/products/mobility-print/installers/client/windows/pc-mobility-print-printer-setup-1.0.250.exe) to download and run the Mobility Print installer.

You’ll be prompted to select the printers you want to use, and to enter your PaperCut username and password.

When it’s time to print, just print like you normally do. Be sure to use a printer you selected when you installed Mobility Print.

<Note>Need to assign an account to your print job? You can do that at the printer or via the User Client.</Note>

<Tip>You will need your WIFI credentials to install and at times confirm your print access.</Tip>
